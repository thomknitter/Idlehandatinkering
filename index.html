About

Hey, I’m Thom.
I’m teaching myself the craft of bladesmithing—one YouTube tutorial, one burnt fingertip, and one crooked knife at a time. Along the way, I tinker with painting board game miniatures, odd projects, and whatever else catches my attention.

This site is where I keep track of the journey, share what I’m learning, and show off the wins (and the fails). If you’re curious about steel, tiny brushes, or just want to see a hobbyist stumble toward MasterSmith, you’re in the right place.

  • Self-taught, documenting the process openly
  • Focus: bladesmithing, Damascus patterns, heat treating
  • Side quests: painting board game miniatures & other tinkering
